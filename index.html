Biography

Sajjad Najafi is an Assistant Professor of Information Systems and Operations Management at HEC Paris. He holds a Ph.D. in Operations Research from the University of Toronto. Prior to joining HEC Paris, he was a Research Fellow at Ross School of Business, the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. Sajjad's current research revolves around dynamic pricing, revenue management, assortment optimization, and operations analytics in the retail and hospitality industries.
